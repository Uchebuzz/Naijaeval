# Code of Conduct

NaijaEval is a community project. We are committed to maintaining a welcoming, respectful environment for everyone — regardless of language background, national origin, technical level, or experience with African NLP.

## Our standards

- Be respectful and constructive in all discussions
- Welcome contributors from all backgrounds
- Focus feedback on ideas and code, not people
- Assume good faith

## Unacceptable behaviour

- Harassment or discrimination of any kind
- Dismissing contributions from less experienced contributors
- Derogatory comments about African languages, accents, or dialects

## Enforcement

Violations can be reported by opening a private issue or emailing the maintainer directly. All reports will be reviewed and addressed promptly.

---

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/) v2.1 as its base standard.
